Sponsor's FAQs

What is the KHELOZI Co-Founder Club?

The KHELOZI Co-Founder Club is an exclusive membership program that offers sponsors the opportunity to become strategic partners and key stakeholders in the organization. As a member, you will represent the brand and share in the company's revenue, playing a significant role in shaping the company's trajectory.

How do I become a sponsor and join the Co-Founder Club?

To become a sponsor and join the Co-Founder Club, please contact our sponsorship team through the provided channels. They will guide you through the application process and provide you with all the necessary details regarding the benefits and commitments of sponsorship.

What are the benefits of becoming a sponsor?

As a sponsor, you will enjoy several benefits, including increased visibility and association with a cutting-edge enterprise like KHELOZI. You will gain access to exclusive networking opportunities, allowing you to connect with industry leaders and like-minded professionals. Additionally, you will share in the company's revenue, aligning your interests with its long-term prosperity.

Can I actively contribute to KHELOZI's growth and success as a sponsor?

Absolutely! As a sponsor and co-founder club member, your involvement is instrumental in enhancing KHELOZI's market presence and contributing to its ongoing advancement. Your expertise, resources, and insights will be highly valued in fostering a collaborative environment aimed at achieving collective success.

Will I have a say in KHELOZI's decision-making processes as a sponsor?

As a co-founder club member, you will be considered a key stakeholder in the organization, and your input will be valued in important decision-making processes. However, the extent of your involvement may depend on the level of sponsorship and the terms of the partnership agreement.

Is the Co-Founder Club open to international sponsors?

Yes, the Co-Founder Club welcomes sponsors from all around the world. KHELOZI values diversity and global perspectives, and international sponsors are encouraged to join the program and be a part of the company's journey.

Where can I get more information about becoming a sponsor?

For more information about becoming a sponsor and joining the Co-Founder Club, please contact our dedicated sponsorship team at sponsor@khelozi.com . They will be pleased to assist you with any inquiries and provide further details about the sponsorship program.
